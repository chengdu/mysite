Business English 900 Sentences

The main issue of learning spoken English is to master the correct pronunciation, this app was designed to help you master the most frequently used words in business scenarios efficiently and their correct pronunciation.

Features:

  • A speaking repeater, a sentence can be repeatedly listening, it's the most positive approach to master the correct pronunciation.
  • The selection range of business materials is comprehensive, informative. Language specification is applicable to a wide range of scenes and situations, mastered these sentences in each scene, you can easily cope with a variety of business applications later.
  • Build-in notebook, for each scene, you can add annotations, such as adding words that you do not know, add the relevant sentences for review etc.
  • Repeatedly listening, grasp the correct pronunciation of each word, the coherent speaking of the sentence.
